  I figured that a 3x3 would help create the image of my anime taste hahaha. This is more of a personal selecltion of either some of my favorite anime or anime that had some kind of impact on my life or just shaping my anime taste. In a way, this basically gives a small glimpse of who I am as a person I suppose. There's really no order in how these anime are listed and organized.

  First off in the top left corner is "Log Horizon". For those unfamilar with the anime, it's essentially a 'trapped in a video game' anime. While this trope has been done many times before this, I personally feel that Log Horizon does justice to the genere by actually accurately conveying the setting of being in a game, and specifcally an mmo. Dispite actually enjoying SAO, it always came across as a pure fantasy instead of an actual game. As someone who grew up playing mmo's, seeing the game's systems that's expected of those games actually have an influence on the world is such a breath of fresh air. Not to mention that the entire anime isn't focused on the single objective on trying to get back to the real world as soon as possible. Being able to live in this world that you've put so much time into has always been something I've dreamed about, so it feels realisitc that some people would actually be happy about being in this senario.
